Air & Grace

A love of heights brought us together 6 years ago at a rock climbing gym. From there I (Alisha) introduced Cara to the wonderful world of circus. Together we are Air & Grace and specialise in Duo Dance Trapeze, silks and arial hammock. We both feel more comfortable in the air than on the ground and love to share the joy of circus through performing.

Alisha

Alisha began aerial arts at a young age and has been training, performing and teaching in New Zealand and overseas ever since.

Cara

Cara comes from a background of dance and Taekwondo. Aerial arts was a new and exciting challenge that she immediately developed a passion for.
